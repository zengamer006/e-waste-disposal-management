e-Waste Defined
List of common e-Waste items
E-waste is electronic products which are unwanted, in bad conditions and also barely functional. It could also be electronic items that are donated or old stocks from the shops where it would be thrown away if not sold. E-waste is harmful to the environment as the toxic chemicals which releases from inside of the metal would drain out when it is not discarded properly.
