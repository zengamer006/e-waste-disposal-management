The aim of this project is

to create an E-Waste Facility

Locator website that serves as

a comprehensive resource for

individuals and businesses looking

to responsibly dispose of

electronic waste. The primary

objective is to promote

environmentally sustainable

practices by facilitating

easy access to e-waste collection

and recycling facilities while

educating users about the

detrimental effects of improper

disposal. Additionally, the project

aims to incentivize recycling by

offering a rewards system tied to

the recovery of precious metals from

discarded devices.

Goals :

 

 

Facilitate Responsible E-Waste Disposal: Create a user-friendly platform that enables users to quickly locate the nearest e-waste collection and recycling facilities, encouraging responsible disposal practices.

 

 

Educate and Raise Awareness: Provide educational resources on the environmental and health hazards associated with e-waste and improper disposal methods, fostering greater awareness among users.

 

 

Promote Precious Metal Recovery: Encourage users to recycle by offering credit points based on the weight and type of precious metals recovered from their old electronic devices.

 

 

Environmental Impact: Reduce the environmental footprint of e-waste by diverting it from landfills and encouraging recycling, ultimately contributing to a more sustainable planet.

 

Monetary Incentives: Offer users the opportunity to turn their old electronics into cash or discounts, providing a financial incentive for responsible recycling.

 

 

Convenience: Enhance user convenience by providing detailed information about e-waste facilities, including directions, hours of operation, and contact information

 

 

Data Security Awareness: Educate users about the importance of data security when disposing of electronic devices, promoting responsible data wiping or destruction.

 

 

Sustainability: Promote sustainable practices not only in e-waste recycling but also in the operations and hosting of the website itself, considering energy efficiency and environmental impact.
